About Coupons

  • To exchange your printed, serial, or mobile coupons with online coupons, enter the authentication number in the Coupon No. Verification box.
  • Only one coupon may be used per purchase. Once the coupon is used, it cannot be reused.
  • Use of some coupons may be limited to purchases of specified items only.
  • Certain printed, serial, or mobile coupons are usable only once.
  • You can only use "Discount on default shipping fee" coupons for the shipping fee of products that are categorized as "Default shipping."
  • You can use "Discount on all shipping fees" coupons for the shipping fee of products that are categorized as "Default shipping," "Individual shipping," or "Supplier shipping."
